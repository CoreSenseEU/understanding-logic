## Types
### **topic** `$tType` { #topic data-toc-label='topic' }
A way of referring to a [phenomenon](phenomenon.md). 

A [topic](#topic) is a [property](property.md) of a [modelet](modelet.md).

- *Situated* in time and space
    - Could also be an interpretation of what is situated in time and space
- May change over time
- Associated with a mission-dependent *meaning*

!!! example
    - object at coordinates: x/y
    - color: pink
    - temperature: 36C
    - type eye, part of face, part of head, part of human)
